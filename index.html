About Me

I am a Computer Science graduate from the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC). My research focuses on applying computer vision and machine learning to enhance medical diagnostics and patient care, particularly in biomedical image computing. My interests also extend to reinforcement learning applications in UAV communication and path planning, as well as AGV operation and scheduling. I am currently applying for a graduate degree in Computer Science or Biomedical Engineering.
