About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC) with a focus on applying artificial intelligence and computer vision to healthcare. My research interests span biomedical imaging, machine learning, and medical diagnostics.

  • Research Assistant, Intelligent Human-Centered Innovation Lab (Jul 2023 – Feb 2024)
